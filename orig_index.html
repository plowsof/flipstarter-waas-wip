Crowd Funding

My projects run entireley off of donations from kind people like yourself. I accept BCH / XMR / BTC. To donate your favourite crypto to me, find something you wish to support below and click Donate, choose your payment method and the address will be displayed. If you would like to attatch a comment to the donation, click the comment check box, fill in the display name / comment and click the create payment address button to get your unique address. Shortly after receiving your donation, the progress bar will update and, if you attatched a comment this will be displayed in the history section along side the amount and you will notice that the progress bar is now the same shade as your favourite crypto! Thank you!
